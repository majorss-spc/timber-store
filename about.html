About Our Store

A Legacy of Trust and Quality in Construction Materials Since 1982.

Our Journey

Kalra Iron and Timber Store was established in 1982 with a simple vision: to provide the construction industry with reliable and high-quality materials. Over the decades, we have evolved from a small local shop into one of Ludhiana's most trusted suppliers of TMT Bars and Cement.


Managed by Nikhil Kalra and Shivam Kalra, our store stands for integrity, excellence, and customer-first service. We understand that your building is more than just a structure; it's a dream, and we are here to provide the strength it needs.


Why Choose Us?


  • 40+ Years of Industry Experience.
  • Authorized Platinum Dealer of Fortune TMT.
  • Wide Range of Certified Construction Materials.
  • Competitive Pricing and Timely Delivery.
  • Personalized Consultation for Bulk Orders.

Our Mission

To deliver superior construction materials that ensure the longevity and safety of every project we support, while maintaining the highest standards of business ethics.

Our Vision

To be the first choice for construction materials in the Ludhiana region, recognized for our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction.

Our Values

Integrity, Quality, Commitment, and Customer Satisfaction are the pillars that have supported our growth for over 40 years.
